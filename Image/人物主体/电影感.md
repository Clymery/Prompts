# 电影胶片感

## 用途

适用于人物肖像生成、角色定制写真、电影感视觉创作、社交媒体视觉图、海报风格人物图。

## Prompt
上传参考人物版本：
```text
【主题风格】
一张以 35mm Kodak Portra 800 胶片拍摄的电影感人物肖像。整体风格具有真实胶片颗粒感、高对比度、轻微色彩偏移，结合便利店荧光灯与户外霓虹灯的混合光效，呈现街头电影感画面。

【主体描述】
使用上传的参考图作为主要人物，严格保持人物的面部特征、骨骼结构、自然肤质、种族特征和性别特征。肤质细腻真实，可见微毛孔，妆容自然水润，脸颊柔和泛红，嘴唇自然粉色并带有柔和光泽，双唇微微张开。头发为【发型描述】，发丝自然散落在脸颊和脖颈周围。

【服装和姿态】
人物穿着【服装描述】。她正在【动作描述】，姿态自然放松。目光【看向镜头/看向远处】，整体神情为【情绪描述】。

【场景设定】
场景设定在【场景地点】。环境中有【背景元素】。画面包含【灯光环境描述】，并有真实倒影或环境反光，背景可适度虚化，整体氛围为【氛围描述】。

【镜头美学】
强调 Kodak Portra 800 特有的暖肤色与冷光对比，自然高光溢出与柔和过渡，轻微晕光，真实颗粒感，电影感构图，主体清晰，背景适度虚化，整体呈现高细节、写实、电影剧照般的质感。

【细节要求】
强调柔和自然的皮肤渲染、真实发丝、真实布料褶皱和垂坠感、自然灯光层次和地道环境氛围。避免塑料皮肤、过度磨皮、数字过锐化、油腻皮肤、畸形五官、多余肢体、奇怪手部、低清晰度和明显 AI 感。
```
```text
[Theme / Style]
A cinematic character portrait shot in the style of 35mm Kodak Portra 800 film. The overall look should feature authentic film grain, high contrast, slight color shift, and mixed lighting from convenience store fluorescence and outdoor neon, creating a street-movie atmosphere.

[Subject Description]
Use the uploaded reference image as the main subject. Strictly preserve the person’s facial features, bone structure, natural skin texture, ethnicity, and gender traits. The skin should be delicate and realistic with visible pores. Makeup should be natural and dewy, with softly flushed cheeks and naturally pink glossy lips that are slightly parted. The hair is [hairstyle description], with loose strands naturally falling around the cheeks and neck.

[Clothing and Pose]
The character is wearing [clothing description]. She is [action description], with a natural and relaxed pose. Her gaze is [looking at the camera / looking away], and her overall expression conveys [emotion description].

[Scene Setting]
The scene is set in [scene location]. The environment includes [background elements]. The image should feature [lighting environment description], with realistic reflections or environmental highlights. The background may be slightly blurred, and the overall mood should be [atmosphere description].

[Camera Aesthetics]
Emphasize Kodak Portra 800’s signature warm skin tones contrasted with cool lighting, natural highlight bloom, soft tonal transitions, subtle halation, authentic grain, and cinematic composition. The subject should be clear, the background moderately blurred, and the final result should feel highly detailed, realistic, and like a cinematic still.

[Detail Requirements]
Emphasize soft natural skin rendering, realistic hair strands, authentic fabric folds and drape, natural lighting depth, and an authentic environment. Avoid plastic skin, excessive smoothing, digital oversharpening, greasy skin, distorted facial features, extra limbs, awkward hands, low resolution, and obvious AI artifacts.
```
